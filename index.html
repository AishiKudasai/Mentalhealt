Self-Care Toolkit

Daily Practices for Mental Wellness

  • Mindfulness: Start with 5 minutes of breathing exercises each morning.

  • Movement: Incorporate 30 minutes of physical activity you enjoy.

  • Journaling: Write down three things you're grateful for each day.

  • Sleep: Maintain a consistent sleep schedule for better mental health.
